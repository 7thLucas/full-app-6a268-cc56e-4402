# XINTERVIEWX

## Product

**Name**: XINTERVIEWX
**Type**: Internal web application
**Category**: AI-powered HR & Recruitment Technology

## What It Does

XINTERVIEWX is an AI-powered video analysis platform designed for companies that conduct structured hiring interviews. It ingests recorded interview sessions, automatically transcribes conversations, analyzes sentiment and tone, detects competency signals, and generates structured evaluation reports — transforming raw video footage into consistent, objective, and audit-ready hiring intelligence.

## Target Users

| Persona | Role | Primary Need |
|---|---|---|
| **HR Manager / Recruiter** | Owns the hiring pipeline | Fast, consistent candidate evaluations without manual note review |
| **Hiring Manager** | Final decision-maker for a role | Objective competency scores and comparative candidate data |
| **Interview Panel / Team Lead** | Conducts interviews | Confidence that every session is captured, scored, and documented |

## Core Features

1. **Video Upload & Management** — Upload or link recorded interview sessions; organize by role, department, or candidate
2. **AI Transcription** — Automatic speech-to-text conversion with speaker diarization and precise timestamping
3. **Sentiment & Tone Analysis** — Detect emotional tone, confidence levels, and engagement patterns across the full session
4. **Competency & Keyword Detection** — Identify and score candidate responses against custom or predefined competency frameworks
5. **Candidate Scoring** — Automated, rubric-based scoring with support for side-by-side candidate comparison
6. **Interview Reports** — Auto-generated structured reports with highlighted quotes, competency scores, sentiment summary, and hiring recommendations
7. **Dashboard & Pipeline View** — Visual overview of all sessions, candidate status, role pipeline, and team-level hiring metrics

## Positioning

Interview Analyzer is an internal enterprise tool that acts as the intelligent backbone of a company's hiring process. It replaces subjective, inconsistent, note-heavy evaluations with data-backed analysis — reducing interviewer bias, accelerating decision-making, and creating a fully auditable record for every hiring decision.

**Key differentiator**: It does not replace human judgment — it removes the noise around it, so hiring teams focus on what matters most.

- **3× more consistent** evaluations across different interviewers
- **40% faster** time-to-hiring decision

## Strategic Principles

1. **Objectivity first** — AI insights augment, not replace, human judgment; reduce bias through consistent, rubric-based analysis
2. **Speed without sacrifice** — Surface key moments in minutes, not hours of manual review
3. **Auditability** — Every evaluation decision is traceable, timestamped, and documented
4. **Ease of adoption** — Designed for HR teams, not data scientists; zero-friction onboarding and intuitive UX
