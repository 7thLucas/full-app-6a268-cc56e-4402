# XINTERVIEWX

## Product Summary
A personal command center for a company owner/manager to analyze interview sessions. Upload video recordings of interviews and instantly get AI-powered breakdowns — quality scores, key moments, candidate communication insights, and overall hiring intelligence.

## Target User
Single user: the company owner or hiring manager who currently has zero visibility into how their team's interviews are actually going.

## Core Problem
No insight into interview quality across the company. Decisions made on gut feel. Recordings pile up unwatched.

## Day-One Feature
Upload an interview video → receive an AI analysis report: interview quality rating, key question/answer moments, candidate assessment signals, and a summary of how the interview went.

## Brand
- Name: XINTERVIEWX
- Tone: Bold, analytical, decisive. A tool for someone who wants the truth about their hiring process.
- Anti-references: No fluffy HR-speak. No generic dashboards. Sharp, specific, actionable output.
