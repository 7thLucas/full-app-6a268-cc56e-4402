# Design Guidelines — XINTERVIEWX

## Personality
Bold and data-forward. This is a power tool for a decisive operator — not a consumer wellness app.

## Colors
- Primary: Deep navy (#0A0F1E) or near-black background
- Accent: Electric blue (#3B82F6) or sharp cyan for highlights and CTAs
- Success/positive signals: Clean green (#22C55E)
- Text: High-contrast white/light gray on dark backgrounds

## Typography
- Headlines: Bold, uppercase-friendly, wide tracking (matches XINTERVIEWX energy)
- Body: Clean sans-serif, high legibility
- Data labels: Monospace or tabular numerals for scores/metrics

## Key UI Components
- Upload Zone: Large, prominent drag-and-drop video upload area — the hero of the home screen
- Analysis Report Card: Clean card layout showing quality score (large number), key moments list, candidate signals, and a written summary
- Interview History List: Scrollable list of past uploads with quick-glance scores
- Score Visualization: Bold numeric score (e.g. 8.4/10) with color-coded indicator

## Elevation & Spacing
- Dark card surfaces with subtle borders on dark background
- Generous padding — this is a reading/analysis tool, not a dense dashboard
- Mobile-responsive but desktop-first
